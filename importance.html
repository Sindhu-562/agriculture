Importance of Agriculture

Why Agriculture Matters

Agriculture is the backbone of human civilization. It provides food, raw materials, and employment, supporting billions of lives worldwide.

Key benefits of agriculture include:

  • Ensuring global food security and nutrition.
  • Providing raw materials for industries like textiles and pharmaceuticals.
  • Boosting economies through exports and local trade.
  • Preserving biodiversity and combating climate change through sustainable practices.
